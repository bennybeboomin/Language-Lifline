Why this work matters

When a language fades, so do histories, knowledge systems, and unique ways of seeing the world. Our club partners with speakers and community groups to document stories, share interviews, and mobilize support for revitalization initiatives.

Community‑first

We center consent, collaboration, and benefit to speakers. Materials are shared only with permission.

Open access

Interviews are published for learning and advocacy, with clear context and attributions.

Actionable

Each story includes ways to help—donate to local orgs, volunteer, or simply learn and share.

Respectful

We avoid extractive approaches. Speakers approve edits and can retract at any time.
