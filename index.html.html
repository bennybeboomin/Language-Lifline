EqualMath

Free, student-led math tutoring for underserved youth worldwide

Our Mission

EqualMath provides free, personalized math tutoring to students from low-income backgrounds, refugee communities, or anyone lacking access to quality math education. Our volunteer tutors are passionate high school and college students who want to make a difference.

How It Works

1. Sign Up

Students (or parents/teachers) fill out a simple sign-up form to request tutoring help.

2. Get Matched

We match students with a qualified tutor based on their grade, needs, and learning style.

3. Learn Weekly

Students attend free weekly Zoom sessions and receive support and mentorship from their tutor.

4. Grow and Thrive

We track progress, offer feedback, and build student confidence in math — and beyond.
